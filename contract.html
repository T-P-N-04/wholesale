Seller Service Level Agreement (SLA)

This agreement outlines the responsibilities of Sellers using the Bhubaneswar Connect platform to ensure high quality service for local buyers.

1. Inventory Accuracy

Sellers agree to maintain 95% accuracy on stock levels displayed online. Any order cancellation due to insufficient stock must be communicated to the Buyer within 2 hours of confirmation.

2. Pricing Integrity

Prices listed must be the final wholesale price. No hidden fees are allowed. Any changes resulting from an RFQ must be recorded and honored.

3. Dispute Resolution

In the event of a dispute, both Seller and Buyer agree to first attempt resolution directly. Bhubaneswar Connect will only intervene to provide transaction logs and communication records.

By registering, you agree to abide by this SLA.
